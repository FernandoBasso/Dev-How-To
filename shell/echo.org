
How to print `-n'?

~echo -- -n~ prints "-- -n".

We could ~man ascii~ and then ~echo -e '\x2d'n~.

“Any fool can make something complicated. It takes a genius to make it simple.”

Therefore: ~echo -n -; echo n;~

Still, jokes apart, the version with ~-e~ and ~\x2d~ is cool too.
