====== column tab as separator ======

https://unix.stackexchange.com/questions/57222/how-can-i-use-column-to-delimit-on-tabs-and-not-spaces

'''
column -t -s $'\t'
'''

We use the $'' shell quoting to mean a literal tab. Or use ^v t to insert a literal tab, but quote or escape it.
