====== Fonts ======
Created Thursday 24 September 2020

__NOTE__: The great majority of fonts look to tall, taking too much vertical space. I prefer fonts that look like they would fit on a square rather the tall ones, that would fit into a tall rectangle.

* Hermit
* SpaceMono
* ParaType (pt mono)
* Cascadia Code — Microsoft
* Source Code Pro/Variable — Adobe
* https://github.com/dtinth/comic-mono-font (shared by phao)
* https://github.com/shannpersand/comic-shanns (shared by phao)
* https://github.com/be5invis/Iosevka
* Inconsolata [Semi]Expanded Semibold is not too tall
